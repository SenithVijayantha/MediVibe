Connecting Patients to Pharmacies, Making Healthcare Effortless.

MediVibe offers fast, secure ordering and delivery for patients and efficient prescription management for pharmacies.

Overview

MediVibe is a web-based pharmacy management platform that connects patients and pharmacies for a seamless experience. Patients can upload prescriptions, manage orders, and track deliveries in real-time, while pharmacies can verify prescriptions, manage inventory, and facilitate pickups or home delivery. MediVibe simplifies prescription handling, reduces errors, and enhances healthcare efficiency.

Services

Home Delivery and Pickup Options

Patients have the convenience of choosing between home delivery or in-store pickup, while pharmacies can handle both options effectively

Prescription Upload and Management

Patients can upload their prescriptions, and pharmacies can easily verify and manage them, ensuring accurate processing.

Medicine Ordering and Tracking

Patients can order medicines online and track their order status, while pharmacies can efficiently manage orders and ensure timely fulfillment.

Real-Time Notifications for Patients

Patients receive real-time updates on the status of their orders, including when their medicines are ready for pickup or delivery.

Order Management

Pharmacies handle orders, ensuring accurate stock levels and timely medication preparation.

Customer Satisfaction and Service

Pharmacies can improve customer experience by providing quick, transparent, and reliable services, ensuring timely delivery and order processing for patients.
